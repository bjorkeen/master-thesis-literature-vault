## What it is
Introduces LIME — local interpretable model-agnostic explanations via interpretable surrogate models.

## Why it matters for the thesis
Contrasts with SHAP to justify design choice. LIME vs SHAP comparison in Chapter 4 strengthens the methodological decision to use SHAP.

## What it leaves open
Local approximations can be unstable. Less theoretically grounded than SHAP for tree models.
