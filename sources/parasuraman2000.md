## What it is
Classic taxonomy of human–automation interaction levels — from full manual to full automation. Defines the intermediate levels where HITL operates.

## Why it matters for the thesis
The theoretical foundation for meaningful human oversight. Your moderate ML accuracy (68%) keeps the system at levels 3-5 not level 10 (full auto). Justifies the intentional accuracy design choice.

## What it leaves open
Pre-AI era framework — does not address ML explainability or CDT.

## Where it appears
