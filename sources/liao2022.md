## What it is
Reframes XAI from an algorithm problem to a user experience design problem — what explanations do users actually need in context?

## Why it matters for the thesis
Connects XAI design to your UI decisions. The human-centered XAI framing justifies why your SHAP output is displayed as a ranked feature list rather than raw values.

## What it leaves open
User experience focus — no CDT or operational workflow with SLA pressure.
