## What it is
Empirical study of trust in AI and its role in technology acceptance — finds trust is multidimensional and domain-dependent.

## Why it matters for the thesis
Recent empirical trust data. Connects trust calibration to technology acceptance — relevant for interpreting participant behaviour in your HITL condition.

## What it leaves open
Technology acceptance framing differs from operational decision quality.
