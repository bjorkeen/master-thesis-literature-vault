## What it is
Foundational paper establishing interactive machine learning — humans actively participate in model training through feedback correction and guidance.

## Why it matters for the thesis
Historical foundation of HITL ML. Establishes the principle that human participation improves model quality — the theoretical basis for the HITL condition outperforming AI-only.

## What it leaves open
2014 — predates modern XAI and CDT. Foundational but not operational.
