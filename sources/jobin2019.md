## What it is
Analysis of 84 AI ethics guidelines — identifies convergence around transparency accountability fairness and safety as cross-cutting principles.

## Why it matters for the thesis
Provides evidence that transparency (your XAI layer) and accountability (your HITL override) are recognised governance requirements not just design preferences.

## What it leaves open
Survey of guidelines — not an operational system.

## Where it appears
