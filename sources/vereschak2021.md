## What it is
Survey of empirical methodologies for evaluating trust in AI-assisted decision making — covers measurement instruments experimental designs and trust dimensions.

## Why it matters for the thesis
Critical for your evaluation design. The survey maps directly to your NASA-TLX and trust measurement items in the participant questionnaire. Establishes what valid trust measurement looks like.

## What it leaves open
Survey covers evaluation methodology — does not operationalise trust measurement inside a CDT or operational workflow.

## Where it appears
Chapter 5 evaluation design. Questionnaire trust items validated against this survey.
