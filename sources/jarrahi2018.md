## What it is
Proposes human-AI symbiosis — humans and AI complement each other. Humans excel at tacit knowledge and novel situations; AI excels at pattern recognition in structured data.

## Why it matters for the thesis
The complementarity thesis is the theoretical foundation of H1. If HITL > AI-only it is because human and AI capabilities are complementary not duplicative.

## What it leaves open
2018 — theoretical argument without CDT or operational HITL implementation.
