## What it is
SLR confirming that the dominant pattern in digital twin AI research is predictive modelling and monitoring — not structured human decision integration or governance.

## Why it matters for the thesis
The SLR itself is gap evidence. Confirms the field has been systematically reviewed and found to lack structured human decision authority integration — a strong claim.

## What it leaves open
Being an SLR it synthesises rather than solves. Confirms the gap exists but does not propose a design to fill it.

## Where it appears
