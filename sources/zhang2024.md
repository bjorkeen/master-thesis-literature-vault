## What it is
Combines XAI with human-in-the-loop control inside a dynamic data-driven digital twin simulation framework.

## Why it matters for the thesis
The single paper closest to the thesis. Proves the HITL+CDT+XAI combination is viable as a research object. Provides vocabulary for dynamic state modelling under uncertainty.

## What it leaves open
Focuses on simulation fidelity — not on structured human decision authority with override rights or SLA-aware routing. Human is observer not governance authority.

## Where it appears
Foundational reference for Chapter 2 and Chapter 3. Directly cited in gap statement. Architecture extends this.
