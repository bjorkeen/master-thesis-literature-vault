## What it is
Proposes transparency by design — embedding transparency mechanisms in AI systems from the start not as an afterthought.

## Why it matters for the thesis
Frames your SHAP explainability layer as a transparency-by-design implementation. XAI embedded in architecture not bolted on.

## What it leaves open
Design principles paper — no empirical evaluation or CDT.
