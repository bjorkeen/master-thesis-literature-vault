## What it is
Distinguishes explainability (what the system does) from causability (human understanding of why) — both affect trust and acceptance.

## Why it matters for the thesis
The explainability vs causability distinction is relevant for interpreting your H2 results. SHAP provides explainability — whether it creates causability depends on participant expertise.

## What it leaves open
HCI study context differs from operational data quality management.
