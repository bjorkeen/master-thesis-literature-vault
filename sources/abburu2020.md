## What it is
Introduces the Cognitwin architecture — a hybrid CDT combining physical simulation with cognitive reasoning for process industry applications.

## Why it matters for the thesis
One of the first concrete CDT implementations. Validates that cognitive reasoning can be embedded in digital twins in an industrial context.

## What it leaves open
Process industry domain. No HITL or human decision authority — cognitive layer is fully automated.

## Where it appears
Chapter 2 CDT section. Historical precedent for cognitive reasoning in digital twins.
