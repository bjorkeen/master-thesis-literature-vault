## What it is
Demonstrates a cognitive digital twin for manufacturing — uses ML to predict equipment failures and recommend maintenance actions.

## Why it matters for the thesis
Manufacturing CDT precedent. Connects ML-based prediction to digital twin state updates.

## What it leaves open
Manufacturing domain. No HITL — recommendations are automated without human override.

## Where it appears
Chapter 2 CDT applications. Cross-domain validation of CDT concept.
