## What it is
IS survey of AI for decision making — evolution of AI decision support from DSS to modern ML. Identifies challenges and a research agenda.

## Why it matters for the thesis
Connects AI decision making to IS research tradition. The research agenda explicitly identifies human-AI collaboration as an open challenge.

## What it leaves open
Survey identifies the challenge but does not propose a CDT-based solution.

## Where it appears
Chapter 2 decision intelligence section. Gap statement draws on Duan's research agenda.
