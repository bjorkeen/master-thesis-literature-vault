## What it is
Proposes FEDS — a framework for choosing and executing evaluation in DSR. Distinguishes formative vs summative evaluation and naturalistic vs artificial settings.

## Why it matters for the thesis
Justifies your evaluation design choices — why three participants why artificial vs naturalistic and why the specific metrics. Directly maps to Chapter 5 and 6.

## What it leaves open
Evaluation framework — no specific domain or implementation guidance.
