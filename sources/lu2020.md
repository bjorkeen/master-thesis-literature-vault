## What it is
Applies cognitive twin concepts to IoT decision support — demonstrates a working cognitive twin that recommends actions based on system state.

## Why it matters for the thesis
Concrete CDT implementation precedent. Shows cognitive twins can support operational decisions not just monitoring.

## What it leaves open
IoT domain — not enterprise data operations. No HITL or human override mechanism.
