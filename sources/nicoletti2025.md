## What it is
Proposes a layered digital twin architecture where human operators agentic AI and machines co-exist in a structured collaboration framework.

## Why it matters for the thesis
Establishes that digital twin frameworks can formally accommodate human–AI collaboration roles. Closest architectural antecedent to the HITL-CDT prototype.

## What it leaves open
Treats the human as one actor among equals — not as a governance authority with structured override rights and SLA-bound escalation paths. No empirical evaluation of decision outcomes.

## Where it appears
Directly informs System Design (Chapter 4). Architecture comparison references this.
