## What it is
Frames AI as a prediction machine — AI reduces the cost of prediction while humans retain judgment on what to do with predictions.

## Why it matters for the thesis
Economically grounds the thesis: your system provides cheap ML predictions while preserving expensive human judgment for uncertain cases. The routing thresholds implement this economics.

## What it leaves open
Business economics framing — not a technical system or empirical evaluation.
