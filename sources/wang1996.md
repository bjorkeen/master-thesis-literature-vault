## What it is
Classic paper establishing the user-centric DQ framework — 15 DQ dimensions grouped into intrinsic contextual representational and accessibility categories.

## Why it matters for the thesis
Canonical DQ reference. Grounding the incident typology in a 25-year tradition of rigorous DQ research.

## What it leaves open
Pre-digital pre-ML — dimensions and context differ from automated data pipeline operations.
