## What it is
The canonical DSR framework — research through artifact construction and evaluation. Defines the relevance-rigour cycle and seven guidelines for IS design science.

## Why it matters for the thesis
Legitimises the prototype-first approach. Without DSR framing building a system as an MSc thesis needs justification. With it the artifact IS the contribution.

## What it leaves open
Methodology paper — does not address HITL CDT or XAI.

## Where it appears
Chapter 3 (Methodology) opens with Hevner. Evaluation design in Chapter 6 maps to DSR evaluation criteria.
