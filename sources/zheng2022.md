## What it is
Defines the cognitive digital twin as an evolution beyond traditional digital twins — adding reasoning learning and decision-support capabilities to the standard simulation model.

## Why it matters for the thesis
Provides the theoretical definition of CDT that the thesis builds on. Establishes CDT as a research-worthy concept distinct from standard digital twins.

## What it leaves open
Visionary paper — does not implement a HITL governance layer or empirical evaluation.

## Where it appears
Chapter 2 CDT definition section. The thesis artifact is positioned as an implementation of the CDT vision.
