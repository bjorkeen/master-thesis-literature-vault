## What it is
Proposes the AI4People ethical framework — beneficence non-maleficence autonomy justice and explicability as principles for good AI.

## Why it matters for the thesis
Connects your HITL design to the explicability and autonomy principles. HITL preserves human autonomy; XAI provides explicability.

## What it leaves open
Philosophical framework — not a technical system or empirical evaluation.

## Where it appears
Chapter 2 ethics framing. HITL as ethical design choice.
