## What it is
Extends Hevner 2004 with a process model for DSR — six activities from problem identification through communication. More prescriptive than Hevner.

## Why it matters for the thesis
Provides the step-by-step DSR process your thesis follows. Chapter 3 maps each step to your research activities.

## What it leaves open
Process model — does not address evaluation methods in detail.

## Where it appears
